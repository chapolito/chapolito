About Chapolito

Jesse Chapo built his first website in '98, a fan site for Starcraft, complete with animated gifs and marquee font. His passion for design segued to a freelance career under the alias of Chapolito where he collaborated with companies like Sony, Mtv, & Qualcomm. His work has been internationally published and received multiple awards, including a Webby in 2011. Recently, he left freelancing to craft the creative vision of Fanpics and help improve the fan experience of live events globally.

He blogs sporadically at jessechapo.com and tweets regularly @chapolito. You can also feel free to just email him at jesse@chapolito.com, he's a nice guy and will respond.
